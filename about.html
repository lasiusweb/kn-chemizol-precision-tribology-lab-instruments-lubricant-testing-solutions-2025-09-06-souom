About Chemizol

Innovating lubricant technology since the late 1980s with global standards and Indian agility.

Who We Are

Chemizol Additives Pvt. Ltd. is a leading Indian manufacturer of lubricant additives, base stocks, and specialty formulations. Headquartered in Bangalore, Karnataka, we serve both domestic and international markets with a strong focus on technical excellence, innovation, and cost efficiency.

Our Global Technology Centre in Bidadi Industrial Area is equipped with state-of-the-art R&D and testing infrastructure, enabling us to develop high-performance, sustainable solutions tailored to customer needs.

We partner with OEMs, lubricant blenders, and distributors across India, Asia-Pacific, Russia, the Middle East, and Africa — offering both additive components and ready-to-market private-label formulations.
