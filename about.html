About Chemizol

A leading Indian manufacturer of lubricant additives, base stocks, and specialty formulations — combining technical excellence, innovation, and cost efficiency since the late 2017s.

Chemizol Additives Pvt. Ltd. was established in the late 2017s with a vision to bring world-class lubricant technology to Indian and global markets. Headquartered in Bangalore, Karnataka, we serve customers across India, Asia-Pacific, Russia, the Middle East, and Africa.

Our Global Technology Centre in Bidadi Industrial Area is equipped with state-of-the-art R&D and testing infrastructure, enabling us to develop high-performance, sustainable solutions tailored to customer needs.

We partner with OEMs, lubricant blenders, and distributors — offering both additive components and ready-to-market private-label formulations with full technical support.

Our Vision

“To be a globally recognized Indian additive company, delivering innovative, sustainable, and high-performance solutions that empower partners and OEMs worldwide.”
